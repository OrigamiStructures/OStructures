Class IdentitySet

IdentitySet manages sets of record IDs, thier context and other details

To pursue a flat style of query as an alternative to deep containment, we'll need to collect and transport sets of IDs to use in WHERE IN (list) queries.

This object self assembles from an Entity, then reports on various contexual details and, most importantly, can deliver an IN list (the IDs of one specific association) for further queries.
