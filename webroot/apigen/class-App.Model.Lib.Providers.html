Providers

Either an Edition or its Formats can provide pieces. When operating on a Piece it is often necessary to be able to access its owner or infomration about that owner.

Making this Provider object encapsulates the logic needed to accomplish these ancestor-focused tasks. It eliminates the data-heavy alternative of placing an owner entity inside each Piece as a property.
